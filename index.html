Hi, I'm Gian, 22 years old, and an aspiring web designer. I'm here to showcase my passion for web design, which allows me to bring my imagination to life. Designing excites me, and I love creating visually appealing, functional experiences. I'm a huge fan of all forms of art, including music, film, photography, design, and nature. Art calms my soul and gives me the energy to get through each day. (Yup!)

I hold a bachelor's degree in Information Technology, specializing in Web Development. Through various projects and activities, I've built a foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which has set the stage for my growth in web development and design.

I'm excited to continue exploring new opportunities and challenges in web design and UI/UX design, with a focus on the design and development phases. My passion for graphic design, multimedia, photography, and videography fuels my drive to create stunning and functional digital experiences.
